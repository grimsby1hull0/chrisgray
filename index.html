biography

Chris Gray is narrative focused sound designer with a history of crafting the sounds from field recordings of anything he can get his hands on and enhancing them to levels of bizarre, unique and engaging sound effects, soundscapes and experimental sound compositions.

sound design

There isn’t a creative output Chris Gray hasn’t attempted, ranging from experiences of live performances, film sets, game productions, animations and audiobooks, even winning an award or two along the way. There are so many stories to tell in so many different formats that he has never limited himself to different working environments, and he always brings something back from them that helps him in different areas.
